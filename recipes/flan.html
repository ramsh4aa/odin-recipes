Recipe of a flan

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups of milk
  2. half a cup of sugar for milk
  3. 1 cup sugar for the caramel
  4. 3 eggs whole and 2 yolks
  5. vanilla essence

Instructions:

  • First heat the milk in a pan and add the sugar for the milk until the sugar comes to a boil and is dissolved sugar.
  • In a separate pan, prepare the caramel by heating 1 cup of sugar until it melts and turns golden brown. Add it to your ramkins.
  • In a bowl, beat the eggs and yolks, then add the milk mixture and 4-5 drops of vanilla essence.
  • Strain the mixture now refinely and pour it into the ramkins with the caramel.
  • Place the ramkins in a baking dish with water and bake at 350°F (175°C) for about 45 minutes or until set.
  • The flan is ready when it jiggles slightly in the center. Let it cool, then refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
